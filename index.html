Our Story

For three generations, our family has been committed to raising the finest quality meats using sustainable, ethical farming practices. We believe that great meat starts with great care - for our animals, our land, and our community.

Every animal on our farm is raised with respect, given plenty of space to roam, and fed natural diets that result in superior flavor and nutrition.
