About Green Pastures Farm

Three generations of sustainable farming excellence

Our Family's Legacy

Founded in 1950 by our grandfather, John Green, Green Pastures Farm began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to raise the finest quality meats while respecting the land and the animals entrusted to our care.

What started as a modest 50-acre farm has grown into a 500-acre sustainable farming operation, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe that great meat comes from happy, healthy animals that are raised with love and respect.

Today, our family continues this tradition, combining time-honored farming practices with modern sustainable techniques to bring you the very best farm-to-table experience.

Family Tradition Since 1950

Our Core Values

Animal Welfare

Every animal on our farm is treated with the utmost care and respect. They have plenty of space to roam, natural diets, and stress-free environments that allow them to thrive.

Sustainability

We practice regenerative farming techniques that improve soil health, reduce environmental impact, and ensure our land remains productive for future generations.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. From the feed we use to the processing methods we employ, every step is carefully managed to ensure the finest product reaches your table.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers and community. Your trust and satisfaction are the foundation of everything we do.

Sustainable Farming Methods

Our Farming Practices

Pasture-Raised

All our animals spend their lives on open pastures, grazing on natural grasses and enjoying the freedom to move and socialize as nature intended.

Organic Feed

We use only certified organic feed, free from GMOs, antibiotics, and artificial growth hormones to ensure the healthiest possible animals.

Regenerative Agriculture

Our farming methods improve soil health, sequester carbon, and create a more resilient ecosystem for future generations.
